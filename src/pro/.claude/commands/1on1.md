## Question Bank

### For Reports (As Manager)
**Career & Growth:**
- What's one thing you'd like to learn in the next month?
- Where do you see yourself in 6 months? 1 year?
- What skills would you like to develop?

**Feedback:**
- What feedback do you have for me?
- What could I do differently to support you better?
- Is there anything blocking you that I can help remove?

**Work:**
- What's going well? What's not?
- What are you most proud of recently?
- Is your workload sustainable?

**Team:**
- How's collaboration with the team?
- Anyone you'd like to work more closely with?
- Any team dynamics concerns?

### For Manager (As Report)
- What's one thing I could improve?
- Am I meeting expectations? Where could I do better?
- What opportunities should I be thinking about?
- What does success look like in the next quarter?

### For Peers
- How can we collaborate better?
- Any blockers I can help with?
- Feedback on our recent project?

### For Clients
- Are we meeting your expectations?
- What could we do better?
- Any concerns or risks you see?
- What's coming up that we should know about?

## 1:1 Cadence

| Relationship | Frequency | Duration |
|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Direct reports | Weekly | 30 min |

| Skip level | Monthly | 30 min |
| Peers | Bi-weekly/Monthly | 30 min |
| Key clients | Weekly/Bi-weekly | 30-60 min |

## Running Effective 1:1s

### Dos
- [ ] Start with their agenda first
- [ ] Listen more than talk (aim for 70/30)
- [ ] Take notes during
- [ ] Follow up on previous action items
- [ ] Be present (no phone/distractions)

### Don'ts
- [ ] Don't make it a status update
- [ ] Don't cancel repeatedly
- [ ] Don't do all the talking
- [ ] Don't skip hard conversations
- [ ] Don't leave without action items
