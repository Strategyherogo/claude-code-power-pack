# Skill: podcast-prep
Prepare podcast episode outlines and notes.

## Auto-Trigger
**When:** "podcast", "episode prep", "podcast outline", "show notes", "interview prep"

## Episode Prep Workflow

### Step 1: Episode Concept
```markdown
## Episode Concept

**Working Title:** [title]
**Topic:** [main topic]
**Format:** Solo / Interview / Panel
**Target Length:** XX minutes
**Target Audience:** [who is this for]

**Key Question:** What will listeners learn/take away?
```

### Step 2: Research
- [ ] Previous coverage of topic
- [ ] Recent news/developments
- [ ] Guest background (if interview)
- [ ] Competitor podcast episodes
- [ ] Key statistics/facts

### Step 3: Outline

## Episode Outline Template

```markdown
# Episode [#]: [Title]

**Recording Date:** [date]
**Guest:** [name, title] (if applicable)
**Duration Target:** XX minutes

---

## Pre-Show (Not Recorded)
- [ ] Test audio levels
- [ ] Confirm recording software
- [ ] Water ready
- [ ] Phone on silent

---

## Intro (2-3 min)
- Hook: [attention-grabbing opener]
- Intro: "Welcome to [Podcast Name]..."
- Topic intro: "Today we're talking about..."
- Guest intro (if applicable)

---

## Main Content (XX min)

### Segment 1: [Topic] (X min)
- Key point 1
- Key point 2
- Transition: [how to move to next segment]

### Segment 2: [Topic] (X min)
- Key point 1
- Key point 2
- Story/example

### Segment 3: [Topic] (X min)
- Key point 1
- Discussion/debate
- Practical application

---

## Interview Questions (if applicable)
1. [Warm-up question]
2. [Background question]
3. [Core topic question 1]
4. [Core topic question 2]
5. [Challenge/counterpoint]
6. [Future/prediction question]
7. [Advice for listeners]
8. [Where to find you/plugs]

**Follow-up prompts:**
- "Can you tell me more about..."
- "What do you mean by..."
- "How does that apply to..."

---

## Outro (2-3 min)
- Recap key takeaways
- Call to action
- Next episode tease
- Sign off

---

## Show Notes Draft

**Episode Title:** [Title]

**Summary:**
[2-3 sentence description]

**Topics Covered:**
- [timestamp] Topic 1
- [timestamp] Topic 2
- [timestamp] Topic 3

**Links Mentioned:**
- [link 1]
- [link 2]

**Guest Links:**
- Website: [url]
- Twitter: [handle]
- LinkedIn: [url]

---

## Post-Recording
- [ ] Export audio
- [ ] Send to editor
- [ ] Write full show notes
- [ ] Create social clips
- [ ] Schedule publication
```
