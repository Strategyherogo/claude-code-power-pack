Reference for defensible email investigations.

## Your Compliance Projects

- **04-compl-mbox-extractor** – Convert .mbox to searchable format
- **05-compl-dropbox-search** – Search Dropbox files
- **16-gws-forensic-product** – Professional forensic tool

## Workflow Steps
1. **Scope Phase:** Define who, what, when, why
2. **Collection Phase:** Export mailbox + metadata
3. **Analysis Phase:** Extract and verify metadata
4. **Documentation Phase:** Create professional report
5. **Verification Phase:** Legal review + sign-off

## Key Principles
✅ Document everything
✅ Preserve originals
✅ Transparent methodology
✅ Acknowledge limits
✅ Get legal review
❌ Don't speculate
❌ Don't delete
❌ Don't discuss investigation
