Create scripts for video content.

## Auto-Trigger
**When:** "video script", "youtube", "video", "screencast"

## Video Script Formats

### Tutorial Video (5-10 min)
```markdown
## [Video Title]

**Duration:** X minutes
**Type:** Tutorial / Explainer / Demo

---

### HOOK (0:00-0:15)
[Attention-grabbing opener]

"Ever struggled with [problem]? In the next [X] minutes, I'll show you exactly how to [solution]."

---

### INTRO (0:15-0:30)
[Brief context]

"I'm [name], and I [credibility]. Let's dive in."

---

### MAIN CONTENT

**Section 1: [Topic]** (0:30-2:00)
[VISUAL: Screen showing X]
[AUDIO:]
"First, let's [action]. Notice how [observation]."

[ACTION: Do the thing on screen]

"The key here is [important point]."

---

**Section 2: [Topic]** (2:00-4:00)
[VISUAL: Screen showing Y]
[AUDIO:]
"Now we'll [next step]..."

[ACTION: Continue demonstration]

---

**Section 3: [Topic]** (4:00-6:00)
[Continue pattern...]

---

### RECAP (6:00-6:30)
"So to recap:
1. [Point 1]
2. [Point 2]
3. [Point 3]"

---

### CTA (6:30-7:00)
"If this was helpful, hit subscribe for more [topic] content.

Drop a comment if you have questions.

Check the description for [resources].

See you in the next one."

---

### B-ROLL NOTES
- [Timestamp]: [What to show]
- [Timestamp]: [What to show]

### GRAPHICS NEEDED
- [Description of graphic 1]
- [Description of graphic 2]
```

### YouTube Short / TikTok (60 sec)
```markdown
## [Title]

**Duration:** 60 seconds

---

### HOOK (0:00-0:03)
[On-screen text: "KEY TAKEAWAY"]
"[Punchy statement]"

---

### CONTEXT (0:03-0:10)
"Here's the situation: [quick context]"

---

### MAIN POINT (0:10-0:45)
"Step 1: [action]"
[Show it]

"Step 2: [action]"
[Show it]

"Step 3: [action]"
[Show it]

---

### PAYOFF (0:45-0:55)
"Result: [what they get]"
[Show result]

---

### CTA (0:55-0:60)
"Follow for more [topic]"
[On-screen: @handle]

---

### ON-SCREEN TEXT
0:00 - "KEY TAKEAWAY"
0:10 - "Step 1"
0:25 - "Step 2"
0:40 - "Step 3"
```

### Podcast/Interview
```markdown
## Episode: [Title]

**Guest:** [Name, Title]
**Duration:** [estimate]

---

### PRE-INTERVIEW
[Brief context/research notes]

---

### INTRO (2 min)
"Welcome to [podcast]. I'm [host].

Today I'm talking with [guest], who [credibility].

[Guest], welcome."

---

### SEGMENT 1: Background (5 min)
- "Tell us about your journey to [current role]"
- Follow-up: [specific question from research]

---

### SEGMENT 2: Main Topic (15 min)
- "Let's talk about [main topic]. What's [key question]?"
- "Can you give an example?"
- "What do most people get wrong about this?"

---

### SEGMENT 3: Practical (10 min)
- "For someone starting out, what would you recommend?"
- "What tools/resources do you suggest?"
- "What's one thing they can do this week?"

---

### RAPID FIRE (3 min)
- "Best book you've read recently?"
- "One piece of advice for your younger self?"
- "Where can people find you?"

---

### OUTRO (1 min)
"Thanks so much for joining.

Listeners, if you enjoyed this, subscribe and leave a review.

Until next time."
```

## Video Script Tips
```
✅ Do:
- Write for speaking (read aloud)
- Keep sentences short
- Include visual cues
- Time each section
- Plan B-roll shots

❌ Don't:
- Write like an essay
- Use complex sentences
- Forget transitions
- Skip the hook
- Ignore pacing
```
