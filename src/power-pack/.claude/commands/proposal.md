# Skill: proposal
Generate consulting proposals and SOWs.

## Auto-Trigger
**When:** "proposal", "quote", "SOW", "scope of work", "estimate"

## Proposal Workflow

### Step 1: Discovery Questions
```
1. What problem are you trying to solve?
2. What does success look like?
3. What's your timeline?
4. What's your budget range?
5. Who are the stakeholders?
6. What have you tried so far?
7. Any technical constraints?
```

### Step 2: Scope Definition
```markdown
## Scope

### In Scope
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]

### Out of Scope
- [Explicitly excluded item 1]
- [Explicitly excluded item 2]

### Assumptions
- [Assumption 1]
- [Assumption 2]
```

### Step 3: Generate Proposal

## Proposal Template

```markdown
# Proposal: [Project Name]

**Prepared for:** [Client Name]
**Prepared by:** TechConcepts
**Date:** [date]
**Valid until:** [date + 30 days]

---

## Executive Summary

[2-3 sentences on the problem and proposed solution]

---

## Understanding

[Restate the client's problem/need in your own words to show understanding]

---

## Proposed Solution

### Approach
[High-level approach description]

### Deliverables
1. **[Deliverable 1]** - [description]
2. **[Deliverable 2]** - [description]
3. **[Deliverable 3]** - [description]

### Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Deliverables |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Discovery | 1 week | Requirements doc |
| Development | X weeks | [features] |
| Testing | 1 week | QA sign-off |
| Deployment | 1 week | Production launch |

**Total duration:** X weeks

---

## Investment

### Option A: Fixed Price
**€XX,XXX** - Full project delivery

Includes:
- All deliverables listed above
- X rounds of revisions
- 30 days post-launch support

### Option B: Time & Materials
**€XXX/hour** - Estimated XX-XX hours

Best for:
- Evolving requirements
- Ongoing collaboration
- Flexible scope

---

## Why TechConcepts

- [Relevant experience 1]
- [Relevant experience 2]
- [Unique value prop]

---

## Next Steps

1. Review proposal
2. Schedule call to discuss questions
3. Sign agreement
4. Kickoff within [X days]

---

**Contact:**
[Your name]
[Email]
[Phone]
```

## Pricing Guidelines

| Service Type | Range |
|--------------|-------|
| Strategy/Advisory | €200-300/hour |
| Technical Implementation | €150-200/hour |
| AI/ML Development | €175-250/hour |
| Training/Workshop | €2,000-5,000/day |

## Proposal Checklist
- [ ] Client problem clearly stated
- [ ] Solution directly addresses problem
- [ ] Deliverables are specific and measurable
- [ ] Timeline is realistic
- [ ] Pricing has options
- [ ] Next steps are clear
- [ ] Proofread for errors
